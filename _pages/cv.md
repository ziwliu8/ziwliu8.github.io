<section class="cv-section">
  <h2>Publications & Preprints</h2>
  <div class="cv-publication">
    <h3><a href="https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.11447">Conditional Memory Enhanced Item Representation for Generative Recommendation</a> (ComeIR)</h3>
    <p>Preprint, arXiv, May 2026.</p>
    <ul>
      <li>Identifies bottlenecks in current decoupled two-stage generative recommendation frameworks and leverages engram memory for stronger GR input and decoding.</li>
    </ul>
  </div>
  <div class="cv-publication">
    <h3><a href="http://arxiv.org/abs/2512.10388">The Best of the Two Worlds: Harmonizing Semantic and Hash IDs for Sequential Recommendation</a> (H2Rec)</h3>
    <p>Accepted by KDD'26 ADS Track, May 2026.</p>
    <ul>
      <li>Harmonizes semantic IDs with hash IDs to address collaborative overwhelming in sequential recommendation, with deployment at Red Book (Xiao Hong Shu) and reported commercial gains.</li>
    </ul>
  </div>
  <div class="cv-publication">
    <h3><a href="https://arxiv.org/abs/2511.19931">LLM-EDT: Large Language Model Enhanced Cross-domain Sequential Recommendation with Dual-phase Training</a></h3>
    <p>Accepted by SIGIR'26, April 2026.</p>
    <ul>
      <li>Proposes a dual-phase training paradigm and LLM-based item augmenter to address domain transition, imbalance, and rough profiling issues in cross-domain sequential recommendation.</li>
    </ul>
  </div>
  <div class="cv-publication">
    <h3><a href="https://ojs.aaai.org/index.php/AAAI/article/view/33336/35491">SIGMA: Selected Gated Mamba for Sequential Recommendation</a></h3>
    <p>Accepted by AAAI'25, December 2024.</p>
    <ul>
      <li>Studies the application of Mamba structures to sequential recommendation and introduces a selective gated module with a lightweight GRU branch.</li>
    </ul>
  </div>
</section>
